ETH Shanghai Web 3.0 Developer Summit

ETH Shanghai
Web 3.0
Developer Summit

The ETH Shanghai Committee will hold ETH Shanghai: Web 3.0 Developer Summit virtually on May 20, 2022, for a joint discussion on the impact of Web 3.0 on the future of Internet.


Since its inception, Ethereum has accelerated towards the era of 2.0 after several rounds of upgrades. Sponsored and supported by Ethereum Foundation, ETH Shanghai marks the final upgrade before the merge of ETH 2.0. ETH Shanghai is committed to promoting and encouraging more developers to join the Web 3.0 community.


Web 3.0 is the technological revolution of the underlying architecture of the Internet, and Ethereum has become the preferred network protocol for most developers, playing a key role in the establishment of Web 3.0 standard. The Web 3.0 Developer Summit invites intellectuals and developers to exchange their opinion and professional insights on the future of internet. The Summit will discuss topics on Web 3.0 global collaboration, Ethereum 2.0, decentralized internet, NFT arts and philosophy.

ETH Shanghai Hackathon

During ETH Shanghai Web 3.0 Developer Summit, ETH Shanghai Committee will organize ETH Shanghai Hackathon as a core parallel session of the Summit.


Since Ethereum came on the scene in 2015, it has not only redirected the route of blockchain technology but also brought profound influences on other industries. ETH Shanghai is committed to promoting the Ethereum network and encouraging more developers to join Web 3.0 community, for which the historical significance speaks for itself.


As the core parallel session of the Summit, this competition will continue the spirit of the Hackathon by encouraging individuals to collaborate, create, find infinite possibilities within a finite time limit, and eventually present a brand-new product to the world. All participants will also receive boundless satisfaction in the process. The ultimate value of the two weeks competition may not be to create a perfect product but inspire developers to try their best and to overcome challenges.

以太坊上海 Web 3.0 开发者峰会

以太坊上海
Web 3.0
开发者峰会

ETH Shanghai Web 3.0 Developer Summit

以太坊上海 (ETH Shanghai) 筹委会将于 2022年5月20日（周五）在线上举办 ETH Shanghai Web 3.0 开发者峰会，共同探讨 Web 3.0 对于未来网络世界格局的影响。


自 2015 年诞生以来，以太坊在经历多次升级后，正向 2.0 时代加速冲刺。ETH Shanghai 是 ETH 1.0 进化到 ETH 2.0 的最后一个更新，并得到了以太坊基金会的赞助与支持。ETH Shanghai 致力于推动以太坊网络的发展，鼓励更多的开发者加入 Web 3.0 开发社群。


Web 3.0 是一次对互联网底层架构的技术性革命，以太坊则是大多数开发者在 Web 3.0 程序开发中的首选网络协议，在其标准的建设上发挥着关键作用。Web3.0 开发者峰会将邀请业内相关人士和开发人员共聚一堂，就未来的网络世界交换观点和专业意见。峰会将探讨的议题包括：Web3.0 的全球协作、以太坊 2.0、去中心化网络、NFT 艺术及其哲学理念。

黑客松大赛

ETH Shanghai Hackathon

在 ETH Shanghai Web 3.0 开发者峰会期间，以太坊上海 (ETH Shanghai) 筹委会将在分会场同期举行峰会的核心活动——黑客松大赛。


自 2015 年诞生以来，以太坊不仅改变了区块链技术的发展航道，更为其他行业带来深远的影响。ETH Shanghai 致力于推动以太坊网络的发展，鼓励更多的开发者加入 Web 3.0 开发社群，其中历史意义不言而喻。


作为峰会的核心活动，黑客松大赛将延续黑客松精神——鼓励组员们合作，共同动手创造，在有限的时间内发挥无尽可能，最终为世界呈现一个崭新的产品。各位参赛选手也将在这一过程中收获无边的满足感。历时两周的比赛，其终极价值或许不在于创造出完美的产品，而是激励开发者们竭尽全力实现和挑战自我。
